PickleJoin Support

Last updated: 2026-01-30

PickleJoin helps pickleball players discover nearby courts and players, share availability, and create or join casual sessions. You can also invite friends and chat around sessions.

Contact Support

Email us at support@picklejoin.app.

Include your account email (and screenshots if helpful) so we can resolve issues faster.

In-App Help

Open the app → Settings → Help & Support to send a message directly from your account.

Common Questions

  • Can’t sign in? Verify your email address and check spam/junk for your one-time password (OTP) code.
  • Location not working? Enable location access in your device settings and try again. Some features are limited without location.
  • Notifications missing? Enable notifications in device settings. If you recently changed settings, try reopening the app.
  • Subscription issues? Manage billing in the App Store (Subscriptions) or contact support for help.
  • Delete account? You can delete your account at any time from the app: Settings → Delete Account. This action is self-service and does not require contacting support.
  • Request my data? You can request a copy of your personal data from: Settings → Request My Data. Requests are fulfilled via email in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
