PickleJoin Support

Last updated: 2026-01-30

PickleJoin helps pickleball players discover nearby courts and players, share availability, and create or join casual sessions. You can also invite friends and chat around sessions.

Contact Support

Email us at support@picklejoin.app.

In-App Help

Open the app → Settings → Help & Support to send a message directly from your account.

Common Questions

  • Can’t sign in? Verify the email address and check spam for the OTP link.
  • Location not working? Enable location access in device settings and try again.
  • Notifications missing? Enable notifications in device settings.
  • Subscription issues? Manage billing in the App Store or email support.
  • Delete account? Use in-app Settings, or email support to request deletion.
